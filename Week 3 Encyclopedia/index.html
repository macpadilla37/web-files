Baseball

Baseball is a sport that is played across the whole entire world. You use a ball with red seams, a bat that is wood, and a glove made of leather. There are 9 players in the field that is called defense and 1 batter that is called offense. There is a pitcher that throws the ball to the 1 batter and the batter trys to hit that balkl by swinging the bat. If we swings and misses 3 times he is out. Once you get to three out you change sides. If the batter hits it into play then the 8 other players in the field will try and get him out by throwing it to a base. There are 4 bases that the batter can run around. If you are on a bag then you can stop nad stay and you are safe. You play for 9 innings, 1 inning contains both teams going through offense and defense.
